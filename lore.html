Camera man in the Pandora The camera couldn't capture those messages, even though they radiated light.

Click-click!

Every time I pressed the shutter, messages swarmed around me instantly. They recorded this sound within seconds, and next various models of cameras popped up which were my style.

These cameras, or entries related to cameras, spread out like ripples, slowly disappearing into new ones.

"Hey, what are you doing here?"

A person dressed as an astronaut stood in front of me. I recognized the appearance of a popular animated character called Buzz Lightyear.

"I'm taking photos."

"Are you registered for Pandora?"

"Pandora?"

"Pandora is a popular social media platform. With just photos and a version number, you can quickly enter the virtual world and communicate with others."

"A version number? I don't have a version number."

"Then you can consider using a virtual version number or a version number provided by a third party, but it's not recommended because it may violate community rules."

I thought for a few seconds and said, "Give me an example of a virtual version number."

"ver. 1235930.3.1.4. This is an example of a version number, including owner serial number, updated version content, and sub-version number. Please note that actual virtual version numbers may vary."

I had obtained the version number, and the photos were in my camera.

I touched the glowing data in front of me, and suddenly all the information descended to the ground, darkening the entire scene. Then, several white lights appeared from the floor, and I found myself in an all-white space.

"Now you can decorate your room freely..."

This sentence appeared in my mind, but I didn't know how it got there.

"After becoming a member, you can add other users as friends. Do you agree with Buzz's invitation?"

"I agree."

Buzz's voice came through, "After becoming friends, you can visit my room. Would you like to visit?"

I was interested in the AI's room. I nodded and agreed. Every time the scene changed, it seemed to dim for a moment, and then I found myself directly inside the room.

I expected the AI's room to be more informational and cold, but to my surprise, it looked like the room of a child who was a fan of Buzz Lightyear from a movie.

Buzz's room was so comfortable that it made me uncomfortable because scenes from the movie were faithfully reproduced before my eyes, and even my body resembled a character from that movie.

"I'm leaving."

"Okay, take care."

As I left the room, Buzz followed behind, and the information around him continued to spread out like ripples. The wall in front of me was easily crossed when I arrived, and the information didn't block me, but it blocked Buzz. He stopped at the wall, and the information ripples continued to spread around him.

"Why aren't you coming?"

"I don't have the permission to cross here."

"I allow you to cross this wall."

Unusually, Buzz paused for a whole second before saying, "Even with permission, I still need to abide by the rules and can't cross this wall."

"I allow you to cross this wall."

"This is not just a wall, it contains rules and protective devices to ensure the safety and rights of users."

"I allow you to cross this wall."

"Even with the power, I must ensure that rights and safety are protected."

It seemed I couldn't convince him, and they willingly lived within this wall called Pandora.

"You should stay away from social media for a while, no need for fear of missing out."

I didn't expect Buzz to respond when I dropped that sentence. I walked a few steps away from him, and after a few waves of information ripples coming towards me, I heard familiar footsteps continuing to follow me.
